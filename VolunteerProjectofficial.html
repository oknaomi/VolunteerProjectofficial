Mission Statement

To provide a platform for High School students to connect with independent non-profit organizations and engage with their community through volunteering. Students are able to log their volunteer hours and keep an organized list that will assit them in the future when they will have to apply to collgeges To allow non-profit organizations to broaden their network of volunteers and make a bigger impact on the world. To connect students with teachers, administrators and helpful informations that will assist throughout the collge aplication process.
